Project 1: Google Workspace Mastery

Description: This project demonstrates the practical application of Google Workspace tools—Docs, Slides, Sheets, and Forms—in a classroom setting to streamline teaching, foster collaboration, and enhance communication. As a tech liaison, I created this training to empower educators with strategies for integrating these tools effectively into their workflow.

Highlights:

  • Google Docs: Showcased how to create real-time collaborative documents for student assignments, feedback, and reusable templates, enabling efficient management of classroom activities.
  • Google Slides: Demonstrated its use for dynamic, interactive lessons and visual aids, including student-led presentations that promote engagement and creativity.
  • Google Sheets: Explained strategies for organizing grades, tracking student progress, and visualizing data through automated calculations and charts.
  • Google Forms: Highlighted its potential for creating interactive quizzes, collecting feedback, and managing sign-ups, simplifying administrative tasks.

This project reflects my commitment to leveraging technology to enhance learning experiences and make classroom management more efficient for both teachers and students.
